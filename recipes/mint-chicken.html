Mint Chicken

Description

Mint chicken is a flavorful dish made by marinating chicken in a blend of fresh mint, yogurt, garlic, ginger, and spices.

The chicken is then grilled, baked, or pan-fried until tender and aromatic, resulting in a refreshing and savory dish.

Ingridients for Mint Chicken

  • 500g chicken (boneless or bone-in)
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup fresh coriander leaves (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped, optional for spice)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Blend mint leaves, coriander leaves, yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, green chili, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, and salt into a smooth paste. Coat the chicken with the marinade and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Grill, pan-fry, or bake the chicken until fully cooked. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and lemon slices, then serve with rice, naan, or salad.
  3. For extra juiciness, use bone-in chicken or thighs. A pinch of black salt or chaat masala enhances the flavor. Serve with mint chutney or garlic dip for an extra kick.
